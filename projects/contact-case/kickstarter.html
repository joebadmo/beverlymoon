I funded the first run of contact lens cases through Kickstarter. Because of the amazing and supportive creative community, not only did I hit my funding goal in four days, but in the end I hit over 200% of my goal!

I am now working on an online store and will be ready to take orders at the end of January for mid-February delivery.

Please email mail@beverlymoon.com to get on the mailing list (Subject: Mailing List) and I’ll send you a quick email once it’s up.
