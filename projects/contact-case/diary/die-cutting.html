Die-cutting

November 29, 2011

Just visited Vince’s Die Cutting in Portland to get some help on a piece of the packaging. He showed me the die that they are making. I wanted to make sure that the cases don’t move around too much in the package so I’m using eco felt to make sure it’s sturdy in there. It’s gonna take me a long time to cut each one by hand so I’m sourcing out this part of the project. Picking up the result at the end of the week.
