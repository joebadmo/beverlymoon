Colors

July 22, 2011

Picking out colors for the first sample production was not that easy since there were so many choices and I was limited to only 2 or 3 colors. Joe and I took some samples to our bathroom and looked at neutral colors that might work well in most homes.I decided that “Natural Grey” and “Glacier Ice” were the two colors that could really look beautiful on a bathroom sink. Super excited and nervous about the color choices!
