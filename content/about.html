Beverly Moon is an industrial designer who lives in small house in Southeast Portland with her husband. She grew in Jakarta, Indonesia and Atlanta, Georgia. She studied Industrial Design at Georgia Tech as an undergrad, then got a job designing wristwatches for Fossil. She then got her Master's in Industrial Design from TU Delft, in the Netherlands.
